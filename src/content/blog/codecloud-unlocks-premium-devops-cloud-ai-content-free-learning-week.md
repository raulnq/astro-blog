CodeCloud has announced its 'Free Learning Week,' granting complete access to its platform's Standard plan for a limited period, from February 10th to February 17th. This initiative offers a wealth of educational resources across crucial tech domains: DevOps, Cloud computing, and Artificial Intelligence. Users can delve into 174 courses, engage with over 1,000 hands-on labs, and consume more than 5,000 hours of content, all without requiring a credit card or incurring hidden charges. The platform emphasizes practical skill development through a blended learning approach, combining guided videos with interactive labs, enabling learners to build real-world capabilities. A significant advantage is the provision of integrated sandbox environments for services like AWS and Kubernetes, eliminating the need for personal cloud accounts and associated billing concerns.

During the Free Learning Week, participants can leverage structured learning paths tailored for roles such as DevOps Engineer, Cloud Engineer, and Kubernetes Administrator, or explore topic and certification-based tracks. The platform also features a dedicated AI environment, allowing users to build, test, and experiment with multiple Large Language Models (LLMs) using a single access key. Other notable inclusions are job-ready simulations, the CodeCloud mobile app for on-the-go learning, and access to popular challenges like '100 Days of DevOps' and '100 Days of Cloud.' Post-event, all learning progress, including course completions and earned CodeCloud certifications, will be saved, ensuring continued recognition of achievements. Prospective learners are encouraged to sign up early to maximize their access window.
