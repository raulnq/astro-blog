The year 2026 is heralded as the definitive era for AI engineering, marking a critical shift from rudimentary prompt writing and basic API calls to constructing sophisticated software systems utilizing agents, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), fine-tuning, robust coding models, and advanced context engineering. This transition highlights a clear divide between those treating AI as a novelty and professionals integrating and mastering AI within their development workflows. Amidst a proliferation of unverified courses, a recent expert-curated guide offers a structured approach, recommending five core learning paths and additional official resources. These selections prioritize building tangible products, establishing strong AI fundamentals, optimizing agent interaction and cost-efficiency, providing practical examples, and ensuring content remains current with industry practices, emphasizing hands-on practice and iterative learning over passive content consumption.

The recommended resources span a spectrum of learning styles and price points, catering to diverse professional backgrounds. The guide features three free courses by the author, covering AI fundamentals for developers, real-world AI application integration (frontend/backend, security, token optimization), and mastery of OpenCode, a significant open-source coding agent. Datacamp offers structured, interactive learning paths tailored for both developers and data scientists, featuring practical coding exercises, certifications, and extensive Spanish-language support, with plans starting at €14/month. AI Giro by Matt Pocock provides focused content on integrating AI with software engineering best practices, blending free resources with premium, specialized workshops for experienced programmers. deeplearning.ai, featuring courses from luminaries like Andrew Ng (e.g., "AI Prompting for Everyone"), offers free access to course content, with paid options for certificates and exercises, though primarily with English subtitles. A key professional recommendation is the AWS Certified AI Practitioner certification, highly valued for validating general AI, ML, and Generative AI knowledge within the AWS ecosystem, complete with official Spanish preparation materials. Supplementary free official resources include Anthropic Academy's "Clow Code in Action" and the OpenAI Academy, offering in-depth workshops and courses on their respective platforms and tools like Codex and GPT models for specialized tool mastery.
