Developer influencer Midudev recently conducted a live stream from the Don Dominio offices in Mallorca, aiming to review community projects leveraging Claude Fable 5 and Anthropic's advanced AI models. The session, intended to showcase real-world application of AI in development, was initially plagued by technical difficulties, beginning with audio adjustments and quickly escalating to severe internet connectivity issues that impacted video quality, including dropped frames and visual artifacts, prompting community concerns about the stream's stability.

Despite the challenges of an unfamiliar setup and Wi-Fi instability, Midudev engaged in live troubleshooting, adjusting settings, and ultimately resolving the critical internet issue by connecting via an Ethernet cable provided on-site. The core of the session involves an open call for community projects—portfolios, entrepreneurial ventures, and more—to be submitted via a dedicated Discord channel, with project URLs and repositories preferred. These submissions will undergo AI-powered analysis, first with Claude Fable 5, then leveraging an Anthropic Max subscription, with Cursor serving as a fallback, all in an effort to provide real-time AI feedback and evaluation. Don Dominio, the host, is also sponsoring giveaways of .com and .dev domains for select projects, reinforcing their partnership with a custom "Don Dominio x Midudeev" branded shirt.
